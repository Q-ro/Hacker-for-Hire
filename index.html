Welcome to hacker for hire.

This is a small videogame developed over the span of several weekends as a mean to showcase some of my abilities as a game developer/designer. The plot of the game is quite simplistic, you, a tallented yet young hacker gets contacted for a job, but things get a turn for the dark, what will you do ?

The controllers (How to play)

To move, use the WASD keys, move the mouse to look around, click to shoot, be carefull what you shoot down tho.

The actors

Hiko, the hacker

Hiko, our protagonist, a prodigy of computer and tecnology but extremely young, cocky and hyperactive, always eager for a challenge.

Unkown

An extreely sahdy and weird looking ... guy ? ... has an interesting job proposition, with gambling and hooker ! :O
